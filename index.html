Serving the Health of Trincomalee with Dedication

Our Vision

To be the leading regional public health authority in delivering comprehensive, equitable, and sustainable healthcare services to the people of Trincomalee.

Our Mission

To ensure a healthier future for all communities in Trincomalee District through innovation, collaboration, and evidence-based public health strategies, while respecting diversity and equity in service delivery.

Our Core Services

Primary & Curative Care

Coordinating hospital services, emergency care, and outpatient departments.

Public Health Services

Community outreach, vaccinations, and disease surveillance programs.

Maternal & Child Health

Supporting women and children's health through comprehensive initiatives.

School & Adolescent Health

Medical checkups, health education, and psychological support in schools.

Health Education & Awareness

Workshops, awareness campaigns, and digital outreach programs for public engagement.
