Towards a Healthier Trincomalee

Our Vision

To be the leading organization in Trincomalee District dedicated to creating a healthier community that contributes to a healthier nation.

Our Mission

To ensure a healthier community by providing equitable access to sustainable, quality healthcare services while promoting self-reliance, personal responsibility, and healthy lifestyles within the Trincomalee District.

Key Services

Curative Care

Planning and monitoring hospital services and emergency units throughout the district.

Preventive Care

Public health promotion, disease prevention, and community engagement programs.

Maternal & Child Health

Comprehensive services from antenatal care to early childhood development.

School Health

Health checks, dental services, and health education for school children.

Well-Women Services

Medical screenings and health awareness for women aged 35+.
